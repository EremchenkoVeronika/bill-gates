About Bill Gates

In 1975, together with his friend Paul Allen, he founded the personal computer software development company that became known as Microsoft . In 1981, the company created the world-famous MS-DOS operating system, two years later, the Word text editor, and in 1985, the user interface for the system, Microsoft Windows, appeared.


Today it is one of the largest software companies for various types of computing equipment - personal computers, game consoles, mobile phones and other things, the developer of the most widely used software platform in the world at the moment - the Windows family of operating systems.
